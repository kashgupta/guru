# Common Medical Billing Errors to Check

## Types of Errors

### 1. Duplicate Charges
- Same procedure billed multiple times
- Same date of service appearing twice
- Check for identical line items

### 2. Incorrect Patient Information
- Wrong insurance information
- Incorrect date of birth
- Name misspellings
- Wrong policy number

### 3. Coding Errors
- Wrong procedure codes (CPT codes)
- Upcoding (billing for more expensive procedure than performed)
- Unbundling (billing separately for services that should be bundled)

### 4. Services Not Received
- Procedures you didn't have
- Medications you didn't receive
- Tests you didn't take
- Room charges when you were outpatient

### 5. Insurance Processing Errors
- Insurance not applied correctly
- Deductible calculated incorrectly
- Co-pay amounts wrong
- Out-of-network charges when provider was in-network

## How to Spot Errors

1. **Request Itemized Bill**: Always ask for detailed breakdown
2. **Compare with Medical Records**: Check what actually happened
3. **Review Dates**: Ensure dates match your visits
4. **Check Insurance EOB**: Compare with Explanation of Benefits
5. **Verify Codes**: Research CPT codes online

## What to Do If You Find Errors

1. Document the error clearly
2. Call billing department immediately
3. Request correction in writing
4. Follow up until resolved
5. Keep all documentation

## Red Flags

- Charges for services on dates you weren't at the facility
- Procedures you never had
- Extremely high charges for simple services
- Multiple charges for same service
- Charges after insurance should have covered it
