# Medical Bill Negotiation Script

## Initial Contact Script

**When calling the billing department:**

"Hello, I received a medical bill for [amount] and I'm having difficulty paying it. I'd like to discuss my options. Can you help me with:

1. Reviewing the bill for any errors
2. Information about financial assistance programs
3. Setting up a payment plan
4. Any discounts available for self-pay patients"

## Key Phrases to Use

- "I'm experiencing financial hardship and would like to discuss payment options"
- "Can you provide information about your financial assistance program?"
- "I'd like to request an itemized bill to review the charges"
- "Is there a discount available if I pay in full today?"
- "What are my options for a payment plan?"

## Questions to Ask

1. "What financial assistance programs do you offer?"
2. "What is the income threshold for charity care?"
3. "Can I get a discount for paying in cash?"
4. "What are the terms of your payment plan?"
5. "Can you reduce the bill if I pay a lump sum today?"

## Follow-up Script

If initial negotiation doesn't work:

"I understand your position. However, I'm unable to pay this amount. I'd like to:
- Speak with a supervisor or financial counselor
- Submit a formal application for financial assistance
- Explore all available options before this goes to collections"
